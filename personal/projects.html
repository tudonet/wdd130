Projects

I'm a freelance web developer who creates custom websites and applications for my clients. I will talk about some of my completed projects and satisfied clients.

Fitness Hub - Website

One of my most recent projects was the Fitness Hub website, a chain of gyms that offers various services and modalities to its customers. The Fitness Hub website was created with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and WordPress, and has a modern, responsive and attractive design. The Fitness Hub website allows users to find out about the gym's plans, units, teachers, schedules and news. The Fitness Hub website also has an online login, registration and payment system for customers.

Virtual Store - Mobile Application

Another project I did was an e-commerce platform that sells a variety of products, such as clothes, shoes, accessories, electronics, etc. The Virtual Store application was developed with React Native, Firebase and Stripe, and offers users an easy, fast and secure shopping experience. The Virtual Store application allows users to browse categories, filter products, view details, add to cart, checkout and track order status. The Virtual Store application also has a rating, recommendation and customer service system.

Restaurant Gourmet - Website

The Restaurant Gourmet project, a haute cuisine restaurant that serves sophisticated and delicious dishes. The Restaurant Gourmet website was created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP, and has an elegant, refined and inviting layout. The Restaurant Gourmet website allows users to learn about the restaurant's menu, chef, history, atmosphere and events. The Gourmet Restaurant website also has a reservation, delivery and contact system for customers.

These are some of my completed projects and satisfied clients. I hope you enjoyed getting to know a little about my work as a freelance web developer. If you want to know more about me or my services, you can contact me via email or phone.
